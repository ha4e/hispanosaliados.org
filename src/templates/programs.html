High School Outreach Programs

We visit high schools and connect directly with students to talk about trade careers, education, and real-world success stories.

What Students Learn

  • The value of education
  • How to get into trade school
  • Financial aid and scholarships

Trade School Awareness Initiative

We bridge the gap between schools and trade institutions, helping students discover rewarding career paths.

Partner Trade Schools

1. Tulsa Welding School – Houston Campus

Welding, pipe-fitting, HVAC/R, electrical & industrial maintenance

2. Universal Technical Institute – Houston

Automotive, industrial, welding and related skilled trades

3. Fortis Institute – Houston North

Training in skilled trades including HVAC, plus healthcare fields

4. UEI College – Houston

Short-term, career-focused programs in skilled trades and allied fields

5. Lone Star College – Construction & Skilled Trades Technology Center

Public institution; programs in construction supervision, electrical, HVAC & refrigeration

6. Texas Technical Trade School

TWC-approved school offering hands-on training in electrical, HVAC, and construction-related trades

7. Houston Trade Training LLC

Smaller school focusing on HVAC and certification programs (shorter term)

8. Interactive College of Technology

Variety of trade and technical programs

9. Aviation Institute of Maintenance – Houston

Focuses on aviation and related technical trades

10. Houston School of Carpentry

Focused on carpentry, electrical technician training, HVAC and similar trades
