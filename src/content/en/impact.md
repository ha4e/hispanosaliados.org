As a new organization, we are just beginning our journey to make a lasting impact on Hispanic youth through education and skilled trades.

## Our Goals

- Reach Hispanic high school students with information about trade careers
- Connect students with trade school opportunities
- Provide scholarships to support student education
- Build partnerships with schools, trade institutions, and businesses

## Future Impact

As we grow, we will track and share our impact through:
- Number of students reached
- Scholarships awarded
- Schools partnered
- Success stories and testimonials
