title: Get Involved
---

There are many ways to support Hispanos Aliados for Education and help empower Hispanic youth through education and skilled trades.

## Ways to Participate

- **Volunteer**: Help at school events or mentor students
- **Partner With Us**: Schools, companies, and trade institutions can collaborate
- **Sponsor a Scholarship**: Businesses and individuals can fund student education
- **Donate**: Contributions directly support outreach and scholarships
- **Share Our Mission**: Encourage social media engagement and word of mouth
